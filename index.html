Thinh Hoa (North) railway, one of four main lines in the North, runs from Hai Duong to Van Don. Originally, the railway had a total of 20 stations. However, seven were in the Son Tay complex and two at the localities of Phu Xuan and Loc Ninh, which were demolished when the complex was built. Ho Chi Minh City has three mass rapid transit (MRT) lines (Line 2, Line 3, Line 5) and six conventional metro lines (Line 6, Line 7, Line 8, Line 10, Line 11 and Line 13). The 6 metro lines together carry about 65% of the mass transit ridership in the city. Ho Chi Minh City is served by the largest airport in Vietnam, Tan Son Nhat International Airport, which has a total of 18.3 km (11.3 mi) of runways.
